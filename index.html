Explore the world of Dreamland!

Cube Faces is a co-op, adventure, platformer for 2 - 4 players. Filled with unique challenges, mini-games and puzzles! Collect moons, try on new outfits, and find hidden treats as you and your friends explore the sleepy world of Dreamland!

Explore the different parts of Dreamland, each area within Dreamland contains 10+ levels, with special twists, mini-games, and platforming scenarios!

FEATURES

  • Dying transforms you into a ghost giving you another chance, but be fast! Running out of time might fail you the level. Quickly touch a living player to come back to life!
  • Moons are rewarded at the ends of levels, work together and collect them all! Use moons to progress through the world, unlocking new areas.
  • Customize your Cube Face with unique skins and hats! New cosmetics are unlocked by finding coins and using them at the store! With each new world unlocked, new skins and hats are added to the store!
